About WAM


Hello and welcome to WAM!

My name is Peachy Bywater and I was diagnosed with General Anxiety Disorder about 5 years ago. This website serves as an online journal of my mental wellness journey, and I'm so glad you're here!

When I received my formal diagnosis, I was devastated and relieved at the same time. My anxiety was manifesting as anger, and it was affecting my relationships with my family and friends. I was glad to finally figure out what was going on with me, but I was scared of how that might affect everything else in my life.

It took two years of therapy, being on medication and the constant support of my family and friends to be where I am at right now. And where I am at in my life is not perfect, but being aware of the status of my mental health has made a huge difference in my life. Here you can find information about my favorite podcasts that talk about mental health issues, book recommendations that has made a difference, and an opportunity to find people on the same boat (or just want to help them paddle along) as you are.
